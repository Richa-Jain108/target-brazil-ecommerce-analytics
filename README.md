# Target Brazil  E-Commerce Analytics

## Overview

Analyzed **100,000+ Brazilian e-commerce orders (2016–2018)** using **SQL** and **Google BigQuery** to uncover insights related to customer behavior, revenue growth, payment preferences, freight costs, logistics performance,payment trends, delivery performance, and business growth opportunities.

This project simulates a real-world business case where a Data Analyst at Target Brazil is tasked with transforming transactional data into actionable business recommendations.

## Key Business Insights

### Market Reach

* Customers placed orders from **4,119 cities** across **all 27 Brazilian states**.
* Approximately **1,451 cities recorded no purchases**, revealing untapped growth opportunities.

### Growth Trends

* Strong order growth was observed throughout 2017.
* November experienced the highest demand, reflecting seasonal and promotional shopping behavior.
* Order value increased by approximately **137% between Jan–Aug 2017 and Jan–Aug 2018**.

### Customer Behavior

* The majority of purchases occurred during the **Afternoon (13:00–18:00)**.
* Night-time shopping represented the second most active purchasing period.

### Payment Analysis

* Credit cards dominated transaction volume.
* Installment-based purchasing was widely adopted across customers.

### Logistics Performance

* Southern and Southeastern states achieved the fastest delivery performance.
* Northern states experienced higher freight costs and longer delivery durations.

---

## Strategic Recommendations

* Expand marketing efforts in underpenetrated cities and states.
* Improve logistics coverage in regions with high freight costs and delivery delays.
* Increase inventory planning ahead of seasonal demand peaks such as Black Friday.
* Continue promoting installment-based payment options to support customer purchasing behavior.
* Optimize warehouse placement and shipping routes to reduce logistics costs and improve delivery performance.
